About Us

DWITI Pharmaceuticals is a registered pharmaceutical supplier since 2013, dealing in high-grade contrast media, medical equipment, and industrial products. We proudly operate across India, various African countries, Israel, Nepal, and Sri Lanka.

We are driven by a mission to make healthcare accessible and affordable globally. Our reliable vendor chain and dedicated logistics make us the preferred partner for hospitals and industries.

Our expertise spans product sourcing, regulatory compliance, large-scale packaging, and custom delivery models for institutions and resellers.

Leadership: Rajesh P. Bhanushali (M.S. Finance, UK) and Velji K. Mange (B.Com, Mumbai) lead the company with over 30 years of combined experience in the field.
